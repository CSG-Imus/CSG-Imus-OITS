About

The Central Student Government (CSG) — Imus Campus represents the student voice and leads campus initiatives that promote student welfare, campus life, and transparent governance. The CSG plans and executes projects, maintains records of resolutions and minutes, and collaborates with campus stakeholders to ensure student interests are represented. Our work focuses on accountability, inclusivity, and service to the student body.
