About CSG

The Central Student Government (CSG) — Imus Campus is here to listen, serve, and make sure every student is seen. We build programs that strengthen campus life and student welfare, keep clear records of our work, and partner with campus offices so your needs are heard. Everything we do is anchored on accountability, inclusivity, and genuine service to the student body.

Para sa Kabsuhenyos, Puso ang magiging sentro ng serbisyo. PUSO is more than a slogan—it is the posture we choose every day. With heart at the center, we speak with kindness to understand concerns, lend help quickly when needs arise, and listen deeply so we can serve better. A heart to serve means we communicate more openly, help more consistently, understand more fully, and do more for our constituents because every action is guided by genuine care for Kabsuhenyos.
